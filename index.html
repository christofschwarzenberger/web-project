psychology

User Experience

UX is how a person feels when interacting with a system. It tries to fulfill the user needs in order to evoke positive emotions such as joy of use so that a positive experience is created. A meaningful UX ensures that your customers stay loyal to your products than looking to your competition.

engineering

Usability Engineering

Usability is how easy, effective and efficient a person can accomplish a given task with your product. Usability is the part of the UX that focuses on the interactions with a system. Optimizing the user interface and eliminating usage problems are the main goals of usability to keep improving products.

VR adds the third dimension to traditional digital experiences. VR glasses immerse the user in a new experience that completely surrounds them. It is desirable if the user feels present in the virtual world. Besides the gaming industry, VR is used in education, entertainment and industry 4.0.

Latest Projects

Virtual Reality in Cars

Thesis, Augmented Virtuality, User Experience

More and more car manufacturers are introducing new entertainment features that transform the vehicle into an experience space. Different approaches integrating Virtual Reality inside the car. In order to ensure a positive user experience the technologies must be adapted for this purpose and passenger needs must be taken into account. While isolation is essential for Virtual Reality to create a sense of presence, passengers have the need to be aware of their surroundings while driving.

Bucket Map

App Development, Flutter

The Bucket Map App, developed with the cross-platform Flutter, serves as a digital travel companion that can be used to mark countries on a world map that the user has visited and would like to visit in the future. In addition, various places such as hotels, beaches, clubs or restaurants can be saved as pins. The pins are used to find places again that you have saved in Bucket Map.

VR Space Invaders 3D

Virtual Reality

VR Space Invaders is a new interpretation of the shoot-'em-up game Space Invaders that was developed by Tomoshiro Nishikado in 1978. The player controls two cannons and a shield to fend off enemies from a virtual cockpit.

Hidden Cultures

User Experience, Augmented Reality

Hidden Cultures is an augmented reality concept in which the user can discover and navigate to hidden places with the help of an app. The concept was developed potential users and implemented as a high fidelity prototype.
